Founding Partners

These extraordinary individuals and organizations have made the boldest commitment to A.M.P. Productions — helping lay the foundation for a legacy of music, philanthropy, and community for generations to come.

Founding Partner status is reserved for our Symphony Circle ($50,000+) and Legacy ($25,000–$49,999) sponsors — those whose generosity doesn't just support an event, but helps shape the future of A.M.P. Productions itself.

Their contributions fund not only first-class performances, but lasting opportunities for students, artists, and communities throughout the Willamette Valley.
