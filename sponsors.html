Partnership & Giving

Sponsorship Opportunities

Join us in amplifying the impact of music on our community. Your sponsorship directly supports creative arts programs at Lebanon High School.

Sponsor contributions support the operation of A.M.P. Productions. Net proceeds from events directly benefit creative arts programs at Lebanon High School. Every dollar invested in our mission is a dollar invested in the next generation of artists, musicians, and performers.

Giving Levels

Sponsor Tiers

🏆 Presenting Sponsor
$5,000+

Presenting Sponsors provide critical operational support that allows A.M.P. Productions to plan, produce, and sustain high-quality musical events benefiting creative arts programs at Lebanon High School.

Prominent logo placement on all event materials, verbal recognition at every event, featured website placement, and primary sponsor acknowledgment in all communications.
Gold Sponsor
$2,500 – $4,999

Gold Sponsors help cover essential production and operational costs, ensuring events are professionally executed and able to generate proceeds for Lebanon High School's creative arts programs.

Logo placement on select event materials, recognition at events, and featured website listing.
Silver Sponsor
$1,000 – $2,499

Silver Sponsors support behind-the-scenes production needs that make each event possible and memorable for our community.

Name listing on event materials and website acknowledgment.
Bronze Sponsor
$500 – $999

Bronze Sponsors provide meaningful support toward operational and production expenses that keep our events running smoothly.

Name listing in event programs or materials.
Community Sponsor
Up to $499

Community Sponsors demonstrate grassroots support for creative arts and community impact. Every contribution, no matter the size, makes a difference.

Name listing on the website or event program.
