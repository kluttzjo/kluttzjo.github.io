Partnership & Giving

Sponsorship Opportunities

Join us in amplifying the impact of music on our community. Your sponsorship directly supports creative arts programs at Lebanon High School and beyond.

Sponsor contributions support the operation of A.M.P. Productions. Net proceeds from events directly benefit creative arts programs at Lebanon High School. Every dollar invested in our mission is a dollar invested in the next generation of artists, musicians, and performers.

Giving Levels

Sponsor Tiers

🎼 Symphony Circle 🎵Founding Partner
$50,000+

The highest level of sponsorship, helping shape the future of A.M.P. Productions.

  • Premier logo placement on all major event materials
  • Special commemorative plaque
  • Naming recognition for a concert series, scholarship, outreach program, or annual event (subject to board approval)
  • Private performance or musical presentation for a special event, if feasible
  • Recognition from the stage at every production during the sponsorship year
  • Full-page program acknowledgment
  • Opportunity to sponsor a specific performance, musical number, or community outreach project
  • Reserved VIP seating & invitation to sponsor reception or cast meet-and-greet
  • Logo on promotional materials (when deadlines permit)
  • Featured sponsor spotlight on social media
  • Verbal recognition during performances
  • Logo in concert programs & on website with link
  • Complimentary VIP tickets
