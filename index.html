Who We Are

Music as a Force for Good

A.M.P. Productions (AID through MUSIC and PHILANTHROPY) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to bringing people together through the power of music. We create inspiring concerts and community events that celebrate hope, generosity, and togetherness while making a meaningful difference in the lives of others. By combining exceptional musical experiences with charitable giving, we strive to strengthen both the arts and the communities we serve.

At the heart of our mission is a commitment to creative arts education. Net proceeds from our events directly support creative arts programs at Lebanon High School, helping provide opportunities for students in music, theater, visual arts, and related programs. We believe the arts enrich lives, build confidence, foster creativity, and create lasting connections within our community.

Our impact extends beyond the stage. Audience donations collected during our events are directed to additional charitable organizations and community causes, including veterans' groups and other local needs identified by our organization. Through music, philanthropy, and community partnerships, A.M.P. Productions creates opportunities for people to come together, support important causes, and make a lasting difference.

Holiday Concert featuring "Known by Name"
Our annual holiday concert is the cornerstone of A.M.P. Productions and the event that inspired our organization from the very beginning. This year, we are thrilled to welcome Known by Name (formerly J209), an award-winning seven-member a cappella vocal band known for their powerful harmonies, dynamic performances, and engaging stage presence. Known by Name will also spend time working directly with local high school students through a special workshop, creating a unique and memorable experience for both performers and audience members alike.
